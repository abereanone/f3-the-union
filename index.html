What is F3?

Men getting stronger together.

F3 is a national network of free, peer-led workouts built around fitness, fellowship, and faith. We meet outside, rotate leadership, and finish together.

Fitness

Boot camp style workouts that scale for the man who shows up.

Fellowship

Accountability and friendship that carry past the parking lot.

Faith

A shared commitment to living for something bigger than yourself.
